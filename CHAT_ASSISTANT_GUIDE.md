## How It Works

### 🤖 AI-Powered Intelligence

When you ask a question, the AI:

1. **Reads Your Expense Data**
   - Total spending
   - Category breakdown
   - Recent transactions
   - Monthly trends

2. **Analyzes Context**
   - Understands if you're asking about expenses or features
   - Identifies what information you need

3. **Generates Response**
   - Provides specific insights with numbers
   - Gives actionable advice
   - Explains features clearly

### 🔒 Privacy First

- All processing happens **on-device**
- Your expense data **never leaves your browser**
- AI runs locally using RunAnywhere SDK
- No data sent to any server

## Troubleshooting

### AI giving generic responses?
✅ Add more expenses first (need at least 3-5)
✅ Be more specific in your questions
✅ Include numbers: "Spent ₹500" not just "spent money"

### Model loading slow?
✅ First-time download ~250MB (one-time)
✅ Cached after first use
✅ Ensure stable internet for initial load

### Response seems wrong?
✅ Check your expense data is accurate
✅ Verify categories are correct
✅ Ask follow-up questions for clarity
